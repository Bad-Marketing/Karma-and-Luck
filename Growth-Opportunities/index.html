Karma & Luck.

Amazon Growth Opportunity Review

Prepared by BAD Marketing  ·  Amazon Department

Opportunity Summary

Karma & Luck has established a strong Amazon presence supported by several high-performing collections and a diversified catalog. The review suggests there is meaningful opportunity for continued growth without requiring significant catalog expansion or changes to the current pricing strategy.

The greatest opportunity is to maximize the potential of the existing catalog. The review identified five Growth Opportunities that together create a connected strategy to strengthen customer acquisition, improve catalog discovery, reinforce perceived value and increase customer value.

Growth Opportunity 1: Protect Existing Revenue
Objective

Maintain availability across hero collections.

Observations
  • Historical stockouts impacted several hero collections, resulting in avoidable revenue leakage.
  • Current inventory health has improved significantly and is no longer a primary constraint to growth.
  • The opportunity has shifted from resolving inventory issues to protecting existing demand through proactive inventory planning.
Key Recommendations
  • Prioritize inventory planning across the brand's highest-performing collections.
  • Implement collection-level forecasting to better align replenishment with demand trends.
  • Establish regular inventory health reviews to identify potential risks before they impact sales.
Growth Opportunity 2: Expand Customer Acquisition
Objective

Continue expanding visibility beyond branded searches.

Observations
  • Branded searches continue to drive a significant share of customer acquisition.
  • Significant opportunity exists to expand visibility across Evil Eye, Red String and Crystal Trees.
  • Current PPC structure provides a strong foundation but remains primarily focused on individual products rather than collections.
Key Recommendations
  • Expand non-branded SEO across the highest-opportunity product categories.
  • Increase Sponsored Brands investment to strengthen collection-level visibility and New-to-Brand acquisition.
  • Develop a collection-based PPC strategy to support category ownership beyond individual ASINs.
  • Leverage Creator storytelling to introduce new customers to the brand through symbolism, craftsmanship and customer intentions rather than individual products.
Growth Opportunity 3: Improve Catalog Discovery & Customer Journey
Objective

Help customers discover more products by creating stronger connections between collections throughout the customer journey.

Observations
  • The Amazon Store is primarily organized around product categories rather than customer intent.
  • Collections are presented largely as independent shopping destinations.
  • Opportunities for customers to discover related collections throughout the shopping journey are limited.
Key Recommendations
  • Introduce intention-based Store navigation to better align with how customers shop.
  • Refresh the Brand Story to strengthen portfolio discovery and introduce complementary collections.
  • Create stronger connections between related collections (e.g. Protection → Crystal Trees, Bracelets → Necklaces).
  • Develop collection-focused store pages to improve product discovery.
Growth Opportunity 4: Strengthen Perceived Value
Objective

Reinforce Karma & Luck's premium positioning consistently throughout the customer journey.

Observations
  • Premium pricing is aligned with the brand positioning.
  • Voice of the Customer is generally healthy.
  • Opportunity lies in strengthening how craftsmanship, symbolism and gifting are communicated throughout the shopping experience.
Key Recommendations
  • Strengthen premium storytelling across the customer journey.
  • Complete the creative refresh to better communicate craftsmanship, symbolism and product quality.
  • Refine gifting positioning to strengthen emotional appeal and key purchasing occasions.
  • Create a more consistent premium experience across Listings, Store, A+ Content, Brand Story and Sponsored Brand creative.
Growth Opportunity 5: Increase Customer Value
Objective

Increase average order value through broader product adoption.

Observations
  • No bundles identified.
  • Complementary collections sold independently, limiting opportunities for cross-collection purchases.
  • Strong opportunity for intention-based positioning.
Key Recommendations
  • Develop a cross-sell framework across related collections (e.g. Protection → Crystal Trees, Bracelets → Necklaces).
  • Launch Virtual Bundles around key customer intentions and occasions.
  • Strengthen collection discovery throughout the customer journey across the Store, Brand Story, A+ Content and Sponsored Brands.
Conclusion

The review suggests that Karma & Luck has a strong foundation for continued growth on Amazon. Rather than relying on catalog expansion or pricing adjustments, the greatest opportunities lie in maximizing the potential of the existing catalog.

By strengthening customer acquisition, improving product discovery, reinforcing perceived value and increasing customer value, Karma & Luck can build a more diversified and scalable Amazon presence while reducing reliance on a small number of hero products.
